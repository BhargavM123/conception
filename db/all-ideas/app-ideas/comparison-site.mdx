# Create a Product Comparison Website

## Comparison websites help users to find the right products or services from a group of similar ones

The ultimate goal of the people is to buy value for money products. Atleast to the middle class people. Now a days there are many E-Commerce sites to buy products online.

But the problem is the price of the product is not the same through out websites. One site will have higher price and another site will have lower price. The prices are flucating in every site.

So the product, I mean your website site should display the cheapest price online. If a user enter a product name, your website should show the prices of the same product across all e-commerce websites.
